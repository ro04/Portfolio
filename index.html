About

I am a Full Stack Web Developer, transitioned from the Navy as an Avionics Technician. The skills I learned while being in the military (e.g. leadership, problem-solving, teamwork, and time-management) has led to the successful creation of numerous applications.

I am growing every day as a developer and have recently been developing projects using MERN (Mongo, Express, React, and Node) stack technologies. I love taking on new challenges and thrive in an enviroment that constantly embraces change.
